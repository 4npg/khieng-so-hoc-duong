# 02. Creative Concepts Evaluation & Selection

## 1. Three Proposed Creative Concepts

### Concept 1: "Khiên Số Tuổi Teen" (Cyberspace Superhero Shield)
* **Title:** Khiên Số Tuổi Teen - Đã Bật Lớp Giáp Cyber!
* **Slogan:** "Dữ liệu cá nhân là vô giá - Bật Khiên Số, né mọi bẫy tà!"
* **Visual Style:** Dynamic Cyberpunk Superhero Comic style with vibrant neon cyan and emerald green highlights, stylized 3D avatars holding holographic digital shields.
* **Emotional Arc:** Empowerment, heroic confidence, thrill.
* **Key Message:** High school students are digital superheroes equipped with legal knowledge to defeat online scammers.

### Concept 2: "Ranh Giới 1 Chạm" (One Click Boundary)
* **Title:** Ranh Giới 1 Chạm - Tải Tin Hay Tải Hoạ?
* **Slogan:** "Tích tắc lơ đễnh, cả tài khoản bay xa - Một chạm kiểm chứng, giữ an toàn nhà nhà!"
* **Visual Style:** Minimalist Tech-Noir, stark high contrast, split screen showing the consequences of careless clicking versus smart verification.
* **Emotional Arc:** Tense suspense transitioning into relief and clarity.
* **Key Message:** Every tap on a smartphone screen has immediate legal and financial consequences; verification stops harm.

### Concept 3 (WINNER): "Bật Chế Độ An Toàn" (Toggle Cyber Safe Mode)
* **Title:** KHIÊN SỐ HỌC ĐƯỜNG: Bật Chế Độ An Toàn - Làm Chủ Không Gian Số
* **Slogan:** "Nhận diện thủ đoạn - Kích hoạt Khiên Số - Bật Chế Độ An Toàn!"
* **Visual Style:** Futuristic High-Tech Glassmorphism, neon cyan `#00f0ff`, emerald green `#10b981`, electric blue, sleek UI overlay widgets, photorealistic Vietnamese students with glowing digital protection barriers.
* **Emotional Arc:** Shock/Awareness -> Education/Empowerment -> Proud Digital Citizenship.
* **Key Message:** Cybersecurity is a mindset and a system setting. By adhering to the "4 No - 3 Yes" rules and understanding the Law on Cybersecurity 2018 & Decree 13/2023, students master cyberspace safely.

### Justification for Concept 3 Selection:
Concept 3 strikes the perfect balance between modern Gen Z technological aesthetic (glassmorphism, UI toggles, cyberpunk lighting) and legal authoritative clarity. The terminology "Bật Chế Độ An Toàn" directly mirrors smartphone UI actions that teenagers interact with daily.
