# 01. Research & Legal Foundations

## 1. Top Online Scams Targeting Vietnamese High School Students (Ages 15–18)

| Scam Category | Target Audience | Primary Modus Operandi (Tactic) | Psychological Trigger |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Fake Online Job Recruitment** | Students aged 16–18 | Advertising tasks like data entry, reviewing posts, translating for 300k–500k VND/day; requiring a "deposit fee" or purchasing task packages. | Desire for financial independence, easy money. |
| **2. Cheap Game Top-up & Fake Code** | Gamers (Ages 15–18) | Phishing sites mimicking Garena/Mihoyo offering 80% discount top-ups or free skins; capturing Facebook/Google login credentials. | FOMO, greed for digital game items. |
| **3. Deepfake Video Call Impersonation** | Students & Parents | AI-generated 5-second video calls of parents/friends claiming an urgent medical or school fee emergency, pressuring immediate fund transfer. | Panic, urgency, trust in family. |
| **4. Fake School Tuition & Fee Notices** | Parents & Students | Fake Zalo messages or SMS Brandnames posing as homeroom teachers demanding immediate exam/tuition fee transfers to personal bank accounts. | Fear of academic penalties or missing exams. |
| **5. Exam Score Leak Phishing Links** | High School Seniors (Grades 11–12) | Fake web links promising early exam score leaks or mock test rankings requiring OTP or social network passwords. | Curiosity, exam stress. |

## 2. Important Legal Foundations in Vietnam

### A. Law on Cybersecurity 2018 (Luật An ninh mạng - Law No. 24/2018/QH14)
* **Article 8 (Prohibited Acts):** Prohibits using cyberspace to commit fraud, appropriate property, or publish false information infringing upon organizations or individuals.
* **Article 16 (Handling Illegal Information):** Requires platform providers and users to block and report fraudulent contents and imposter accounts.

### B. Decree 13/2023/ND-CP on Personal Data Protection (Nghị định 13/2023/NĐ-CP)
* **Article 9 (Data Subject Rights):** Students have the explicit right to know, consent, access, restrict, and demand deletion of their personal data.
* **Article 13 (Protection of Children's Data):** Processing personal data of minors under 16 requires consent from both the minor and parent/guardian.

### C. Penal Code 2015 (Amended 2017) (Bộ luật Hình sự)
* **Article 174 (Fraudulent Appropriation of Property):** Penalties range from 6 months up to 20 years or life imprisonment depending on severity.
* **Article 290 (Using Computer/Telecommunication Networks for Appropriation):** High-tech crimes carried out using computer systems face up to 20 years imprisonment.

### D. Code of Conduct on Social Networks (Decision 874/QD-BTTTT)
* Guidelines for responsible digital citizenship, verifying information sources, and respecting privacy.
